Why an Island?

"I chose the symbol of an island because it perfectly mirrors my life at this stage: a territory that is not yet fully developed, representing that I am still young and constantly growing. To me, an island indicates absolute freedom where there are no rules to restrict my creativity or my future career choices. It is a self-contained ecosystem that shows how my diverse 'tribes' can coexist within a single landscape. Physically and figuratively, this island suggests that I have the potential to become someone great and build the exact life I want to lead."

What This Map Reveals

This map is more than just a 3D visualization; it represents the connection points between the various "tribes" I navigate daily. By mapping these spaces, I am uncovering how my environment influences my identity and demonstrating how I balance technical interests with my personal community roots.

Curated Memories

Soundtrack of My Journey

Songs to me bring back memories of a certain time period. Each song resembles a stage of my life and reminds me of what is so memorable. The song’s rhythm and style contribute to its overall mood, but more importantly, it’ s the when and where that determines the feeling that hits my heart. The following song choices represent significant moments of my life.

01

I Ain't Worried

OneRepublic

VEX IQ (2025 - 2026)

This song links to the stage of life where I participated in VEX IQ, specifically last season 2025 - 2026. The reason why this song is so closely related to VEX is because it was used as a background video in one of the robot reveal YouTube videos by a great team in Taiwan. But behind this literal connection, whenever I hear the song, it motivates and energizes me to keep fighting in the game. It encourages me to show what I’ve got in the match and “I ain’t worried” when facing opponents or international teams.

02

Passionfruit

Drake

Boston • MIT Camp

This song links back to the US again but in Boston. I went there for 3 weeks of summer camp on AI, entrepreneurship, and MIT professor’s lectures. The slow-paced song calms me down so I can absorb all the knowledge given. During that time, I learned a lot that changed me into who I am now. That period taught me that students from all around the world are already approaching university and it's time for me to push as well. This experience gave me a clearer direction of who I want to become in the future.

03

One Dance

Drake

New York City

This song brings back memories of when I visited the US, specifically New York. It was an amazing experience going to a big city that is discussed worldwide. I remember listening to this song in the Park Central Hotel, looking at Broadway for the first time, and feeling stunned by how vibrant and lively the streets were. Staring at the city at night from the top of the Rockefeller skyscraper made me realize this place offers innumerable opportunities. Like the message its lyrics are trying to convey, it encourages me to lose myself and deeply appreciate the moments.

04

Unforgettable

French Montana ft. Swae Lee

VEX Worlds (2024 - 2025)

The title of this song “Unforgettable” describes the VEX Worlds Championship. My team fought hard to earn our spot. It was my second time qualifying since 5th grade (which was online due to COVID). While we were ecstatic, the trip hit us with a harsh reality check: our mindset was completely different from experienced global teams. We were treating it like a fun holiday, resulting in a disastrous game performance. It taught me that to win at that level, you must go all-in. Fortunately, with this experience in hand, we can try again this year.

05

Everybody Wants to Rule the World

Tears For Fears

England Summer Trip

For all those Skibidi Toilet fans out there, they know this is the theme song for the Cameraman. Coincidentally, during that viral trend, I went to England with my school—my first time traveling abroad without my parents. It gave me a unique sense of independence. Surprisingly, during music class at our host school, the student band group I joined randomly chose this exact song to perform! This journey made me more mature, proving that childhood moments abroad with close friends are rare and beautiful.
